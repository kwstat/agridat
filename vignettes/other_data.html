Other sources of agricultural data

Kevin Wright

2015-07-20

This document lists other sources of agricultural data.

Books

Peter Diggle. Longitudinal Data Analysis. http://www.maths.lancs.ac.uk/~diggle/lda/Datasets/ Pig weight data is in the package SemiPar::pig.weights. The Sitka spruce data is in geepack::spruce. The Milk protein data is in nlme::Milk.

Goulden, Methods of Statistical Analysis. http://archive.org/details/methodsofstatist031744mbp Has some interesting incomplete-block experiments.

Papers

Kenward, Michael G. (1987). A Method for Comparing Profiles of Repeated Measurements. Applied Statistics, 36, 296-308. An ante-dependence model is fit to repeated measures of cattle weight. Data available here: https://faculty.biostat.ucla.edu/robweiss/filedepot_download/87/495

Roger Payne (2015). The Design and Analysis of Long-Term Rotation Experiments. Agronomy Journal, 107, 772-784. https://www.agronomy.org/publications/aj/pdfs/107/2/772 Contains data and R code.

R packages

agricolae has assorted data and functions for analysis.

BGLR. Bayesian Generalized Linear Regression. Has an A matrix (but no pedigree) for 499 genotypes at 4 locations.

BLR. Bayesian Linear Regression. Has an A matrix (but no pedigree) for 499 genotypes at 4 locations.

cropcc. Climate change on crops. https://r-forge.r-project.org/projects/cropcc/

drc is a package for dose response curves.

lmtest::ChickEgg has time series of annual chicken and egg production in the United States 1930-1983.

lsmeans::feedlot is used to demonstrate an unbalanced analysis-of-covariance.

nlraa is for Non-linear models in agriculture. http://r-forge.r-project.org/R/?group_id=1599

plantbreeding https://r-forge.r-project.org/projects/plantbreeding/

SDaA This package has county-level data from the United States Census of Agriculture, along with a vignette to illustrate survey sampling analyses. http://cran.r-project.org/package=SDaA

SMPracticals::barley is from a barley field trial.
